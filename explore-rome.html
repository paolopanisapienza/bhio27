Explore Rome

Welcome to Rome. The city is full of historical, artistic, and culinary landmarks, but this page is meant to collect more personal suggestions from the local organizers and participants.

Near Sapienza

  • San Lorenzo for informal dinners and bars close to the university.
  • Villa Torlonia for a quiet walk.
  • Quartiere Coppedè for a short architectural detour.

Classical Rome

  • Colosseum, Roman Forum, and Palatine Hill.
  • Pantheon, Piazza Navona, and Campo de’ Fiori.
  • Capitoline Hill and the view over the Forum.

Evening walks

  • Trastevere and the Tiber Island.
  • Monti and the area around Via dei Serpenti.
  • Gianicolo terrace for a panoramic view.
