Where to stay

The following areas are convenient for reaching Sapienza University of Rome:

  • San Lorenzo — close to the university, informal, many restaurants and bars.
  • Termini / Castro Pretorio — convenient for trains, metro, and airport connections.
  • Monti — central and pleasant, with good public-transport connections.
  • Nomentano / Bologna — residential, well connected to Sapienza.

Add recommended hotels here once negotiated rates are available.

How to get to the venue

From Roma Termini station, Sapienza University can be reached by walking, bus, or taxi. From Fiumicino airport, participants can take the Leonardo Express to Roma Termini and then continue to the venue. From Ciampino airport, shuttle buses and taxis connect to the city center.
