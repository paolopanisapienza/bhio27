About the conference

Black Holes Inside and Out II will gather experts in black-hole physics, numerical relativity, black-hole perturbation theory, gravitational-wave astronomy, high-energy theory, quantum gravity, astrophysics, and data analysis to discuss the status of the field and the prospects ahead.

The meeting follows the spirit of the first edition and will take place at Aula Magna, Sapienza University of Rome, bringing together researchers working on the exterior and interior structure of black holes, their dynamics, their observational signatures, and their possible extensions beyond General Relativity.

Venue

Aula Magna, Sapienza University of Rome, Rome, Italy.

Scientific scope

Topics will include classical and quantum aspects of black holes, strong-field tests of gravity, black-hole spectroscopy and ringdown, numerical simulations, compact-object environments, black holes beyond General Relativity, black-hole thermodynamics, and connections with high-energy theory and cosmology.
